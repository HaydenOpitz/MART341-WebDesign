Future travel Dreams

Being in college is a struggle in many ways especially when it comes to traveling the world. Here are some of my dream spots and things I want to see for myself whenever I get the chance.

Things I Want to See:

  • Christ the Redeemer: This is a main focus towards my beliefs and religion as to see one of the largest statues in the world in the form of Christ is truley a dream of mine.

Favorite Places I Have Been:

  1. Planet Holleywood Cancun: This was my senior trip spot and we all had a blast with great service and people all around for an entire wek straight!
  2. Walt DisneyLand: Went when I was pretty younger but still remember how amazing (but expensive) this place was. Definetly would want to go again soon!
  3. Walt Disney World: Honestly not my favorite spot. It was too much travel time between worlds and felt like they were all too far apart from one another.
  4. Silverwood: Honestly this place is very underrated, it is cheap for having a water park and amusment park all in one and is my go to spot for a quick weekend trip with friends.
  5. Cabo, Mexico: Was a great time from the beach, local shops, to the local food. I would say this is the most beautiful part of Mexico all together!

These are just a few of what I have seen and plan to continue to add to this list as I explore the world further...
