Autumn Leaf Pumpkin Pie

Celebrate the flavors and beauty of fall with this Autumn Leaf Pumpkin Pie! Featuring a rich, spiced pumpkin filling and adorned with charming pastry leaves, this pie is as delightful to look at as it is to eat. Perfect for family gatherings or cozy nights in, it's an easy yet impressive treat that captures the essence of the season.

Ingredients:

  • Crust: 1 1/4 cups flour, 1/2 cup cold butter, 2-3 tbsp ice water, pinch of salt
  • Filling: 1 1/2 cups pumpkin puree, 1 cup cream, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 tsp each ginger, cloves, nutmeg, 1/2 tsp vanilla, pinch of salt
  • Decoration: Leftover pie dough, 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Make the crust: Combine flour, butter, and water. Chill and roll into a pie dish.
  2. Make the filling: Whisk pumpkin, cream, eggs, sugar, and spices. Pour into the crust.
  3. Bake: Preheat to 375°F, bake for 45-50 minutes.
  4. Decorate: Cut dough into leaf shapes, brush with egg, and place on the pie halfway through baking.
  5. Cool & serve: Let the pie cool before serving. Enjoy!

This Autumn Leaf Pumpkin Pie is not only a delicious dessert but also a beautiful centerpiece for your fall festivities. The rich and creamy pumpkin filling, infused with warm spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger, brings out the best flavors of the season. Topped with delicate, hand-cut pastry leaves, this pie is a nod to the changing colors of autumn.

Whether you're serving it at a family gathering, a Thanksgiving feast, or simply enjoying a cozy evening at home, this pie is sure to impress both in taste and presentation. Plus, the easy-to-follow recipe makes it a perfect baking project for both beginners and seasoned bakers alike. So gather your ingredients, roll out that dough, and enjoy a slice of autumn's best!
