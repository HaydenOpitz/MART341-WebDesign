Autumn Leaf Pumpkin Pie

Celebrate the flavors and beauty of fall with this Autumn Leaf Pumpkin Pie! Featuring a rich, spiced pumpkin filling and adorned with charming pastry leaves...

Ingredients:

  • Crust: 1 1/4 cups flour, 1/2 cup cold butter, 2-3 tbsp ice water, pinch of salt
  • Filling: 1 1/2 cups pumpkin puree, 1 cup cream, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 tsp each ginger, cloves, nutmeg, 1/2 tsp vanilla, pinch of salt
  • Decoration: Leftover pie dough, 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Make the crust: Combine flour, butter, and water. Chill and roll into a pie dish.
  2. Make the filling: Whisk pumpkin, cream, eggs, sugar, and spices. Pour into the crust.
  3. Bake: Preheat to 375°F, bake for 45-50 minutes.
  4. Decorate: Cut dough into leaf shapes, brush with egg, and place on the pie halfway through baking.
  5. Cool & serve: Let the pie cool before serving. Enjoy!

This Autumn Leaf Pumpkin Pie is not only a delicious dessert but also a beautiful centerpiece for your fall festivities...
