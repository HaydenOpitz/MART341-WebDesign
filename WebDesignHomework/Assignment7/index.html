Dream Destinations

Being in college can make traveling the world challenging, but I have a list of dream destinations I hope to see in the future.

Things I Want to See:

  • Colosseum: Standing in this historic site would be breathtaking. From its history to its architecture, I want to experience it all.
  • Christ the Redeemer: A symbol of faith and grandeur, seeing this iconic statue in person would be deeply meaningful to me.
  • Mount Rushmore: Surprisingly, I haven't visited this yet, but it's on my list!

Favorite Places I Have Been:

  1. Planet Hollywood Cancun: This was my senior trip destination, and it was an unforgettable experience with great service, food, and entertainment!
  2. Disneyland: I went when I was young, but I still remember the magic (and the price tag). Definitely want to go again!
  3. Disney World: Not my favorite—too much travel time between parks, and everything felt spread out.
  4. Silverwood: An underrated amusement park! Affordable, with both a water park and roller coasters, making it a perfect weekend getaway.
  5. Cabo, Mexico: The beaches, the food, the scenery—Cabo is hands down one of the most beautiful places in Mexico.

These are just a few of my travel experiences. I plan to continue adding to this list as I explore more of the world...

I feel incredibly fortunate to have traveled as much as I have, but the desire to see more never fades!
