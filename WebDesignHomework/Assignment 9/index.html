How to Clean Dirty Sneakers

Restore your kicks to look brand new with these easy steps!

Materials Needed

  • Soft-bristle brush
  • Mild detergent
  • Warm water
  • Microfiber cloth
  • Baking soda
  • Toothbrush

Step-by-Step Cleaning Guide

Step 1: Prep the Sneakers

Remove laces and insoles. Dry brush excess dirt.

Step 2: Mix Cleaning Solution

Combine mild detergent with warm water.

Step 3: Scrub the Surface

Use a soft brush to clean the fabric and soles.

Step 4: Tackle Stubborn Stains

Apply baking soda and scrub with a toothbrush.

Step 5: Wipe & Dry

Use a microfiber cloth to remove excess moisture.

Step 6: Let Sneakers Air Dry

Place in a well-ventilated area away from direct sunlight.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use a magic eraser for scuffed soles.
  • Stuff shoes with newspaper to maintain shape.
  • Waterproof with a sneaker spray for future protection.
