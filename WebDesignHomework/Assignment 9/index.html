How to Clean Your Sneakers

Learn the best methods to clean your sneakers and keep them looking fresh and new. Follow these easy steps to give your shoes a makeover!

Materials Needed:

  • Soap or detergent
  • Warm water
  • Toothbrush (for scrubbing)
  • Microfiber cloth or towel
  • Sneaker cleaner or specialized shoe cleaning product (optional)
  • Magic eraser (for stubborn marks)
  • Baking soda (for deodorizing)
  • Mesh bag (if using washing machine method)
  • Laundry detergent (if using washing machine method)

How to Wash Your Shoes in a Washing Machine

This method might be faster, but it's harder on your shoes and could potentially ruin them.

Step 1: Remove Laces and Insoles

Remove the laces and any removable insoles from your sneakers to clean them separately.

Step 2: Brush Off Dirt

Use a soft brush or toothbrush to remove loose dirt, mud, and debris from the surface of your shoes.

Step 3: Prepare Cleaning Solution

Mix warm water and mild detergent to create a soapy solution. For extra cleaning power, use a sneaker cleaner.

Step 4: Scrub the Shoes

Use the toothbrush to scrub your shoes, focusing on seams and tough spots where dirt builds up.

Step 5: Rinse and Wipe

Wipe away the soap with a damp cloth. Be sure not to soak the shoes too much.

Step 6: Dry the Sneakers

Stuff your sneakers with paper towels to help retain their shape. Allow them to air dry completely.
